LOTRO visual targets
--------------------
  Plate:     warm gold-silver, sharp engraved edges, not flat grey steel
  Leather:   rich brown undertones under mail and at joints
  Marshal:   subtle Rohan green on trim (horse-lord captain look)
  Overall:   higher contrast than vanilla — reads well with OptiFine + shaders

Run the upgrade pipeline
------------------------
  python tools/download_rohan_bases.py
  powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File tools/upgrade_rohan_armor.ps1
  powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File tools/upgrade_rohan_armor.ps1 -IncludeWeapons

Manual polish (Aseprite / Photoshop)
------------------------------------
  1. Open bodyRohan.png — enlarge to 128x64 or 256x128 workspace
  2. Add engraved horse/eothéod motifs on chest plate (LOTRO Rohirrim reference)
  3. Deepen leather straps; add specular highlights on pauldrons
  4. Marshal set: paint green cloth/tabard accents on bodyRohanMarshal.png
  5. Re-export as PNG with transparency preserved
